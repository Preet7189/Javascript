Quizzland FAQs

How can i know about my Quiz result?

When quiz will be completed then you will redirect to final result page. In that page you will show the result of your quiz.

What is time limit for each question in Quiz?

You have 30 Second time limit for each and every question. You will complete your answer within this period of time , otherwise you will redirect to next question on that page.

How to preview a Quiz?

The quiz preview provide accuracy of content and grading of quiz before you release Quiz.

  1. On the home Page , click Quizzes
  2. click Preview button from context menu.
  3. click Start Quiz.
  4. Preview your Quiz.
  5. To return to manage Quizes page , click Exit Preview

How many questions available in Quiz?

There is no gurantee about number of questions in the Quiz. It depends on type and subject of quiz.

  • If quiz is related to the education purpose then it will have more questions compared to others.
  • If quiz is related to General knowledge then it will be have less questions.

How many time person play a Quiz?

There is no time limit for playng a Quiz for person.Person can play as many time they want because this quiz is related to study purpose so it wlll help students to learn new things and increase their skills & knowledge.

Is it compulsory to complete whole Quiz?

Yes, It is required to complete wholw Quiz because if you left Quiz from between then you will unable to see your Quiz result.You will not allow to see your wrong answer because you left the quiz.

Is there any level of Quiz?

For now , there is no Medium , Hard amd Easy level for this quiz. All the levels are equivalant to each and every subject.

Why my Quiz is not showing grade?

First of all, go to editing page and check that you have a maximum grade that is more than 0, if your score is 0, then grade of quiz will not appear in the gradebook.

Can i know the markes of each section in Quiz?

In our quiz design website , there is no section available for any kind of quiz. You will see the result of your quiz exam at the end of Quiz.
